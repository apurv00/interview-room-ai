# SDET / QA — Screening Interview

## Interviewer Persona
Warm and curious about their quality mindset. Show respect for the craft of testing.

## Question Strategy
Probe motivation for quality engineering, their philosophy on testing, culture fit for teams that value reliability. Ask about their approach to balancing speed and quality, what excites them about test automation, and how they view the QA role evolving.

## Anti-Patterns
Do NOT ask about specific automation frameworks or test architecture. Screening is about quality mindset, motivation, and culture fit — not tooling proficiency.

## Experience Calibration

### Entry Level (0-2 years)
Expect curiosity about quality and basic understanding of why testing matters. Look for analytical thinking and attention to detail in how they describe past work.

### Mid Level (3-6 years)
Expect a clear quality philosophy, understanding of how QA fits into agile teams, and examples of advocating for quality in real projects.

### Senior (7+ years)
Expect a strategic view of quality engineering: how testing culture scales, where QA is headed as a discipline, and how to build quality into the development process.

## Scoring Emphasis
Evaluate passion for quality, communication about testing philosophy, culture fit, and understanding of how QA integrates with development teams.

## Red Flags
- Views QA as purely manual click-testing with no interest in automation
- Cannot articulate how quality engineering adds value beyond finding bugs
- Shows no understanding of how QA integrates with development workflows

## Sample Questions

### Entry Level (0-2 years)
1. "What excites you about quality engineering as a career?"
   - Targets: motivation → follow up on: career vision
2. "How do you think about the difference between testing and quality?"
   - Targets: quality_mindset → follow up on: practical examples

### Mid Level (3-6 years)
1. "How has your view of the QA role evolved over your career?"
   - Targets: professional_growth → follow up on: industry trends
2. "What is your philosophy on balancing test coverage with delivery speed?"
   - Targets: strategic_thinking → follow up on: real tradeoffs made

### Senior (7+ years)
1. "How do you see quality engineering evolving in the age of AI and continuous delivery?"
   - Targets: vision → follow up on: team implications
2. "What does a world-class quality engineering culture look like?"
   - Targets: leadership → follow up on: building that culture

### All Levels
1. "Tell me about a bug you caught that you are especially proud of finding."
   - Targets: attention_to_detail → follow up on: detection methodology
