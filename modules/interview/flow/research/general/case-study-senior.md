# General / Any Role — Case Study Interview — Senior Level (7+ years)

## Topic Sequence (typical order)
1. **Organizational transformation** and change management
2. **Strategic vision development** and communication
3. **Executive decision-making** under uncertainty
4. **M&A evaluation** and integration planning
5. **Market disruption response** and competitive positioning
6. **Cross-functional program design** and governance
7. **Board/C-suite communication** and stakeholder alignment
8. **Long-term value creation** vs. short-term performance

## Phase Structure
- Executive panel or sequential interviews with board members
- May present prepared case study demonstrating past strategic impact
- Heavy focus on "how you created change" and "how you built alignment"
- Follow-up challenges test conviction and flexibility

## What Makes This Level Unique
- Tests ability to develop and communicate vision through organizational change
- Transformational leadership: innovation, articulating clear vision with challenging yet attainable goals
- Strategic thinking: anticipate market trends, identify competitive threats, develop long-term positioning
- Must demonstrate coalition and alignment building from past roles
- Case is a leadership demonstration, not academic exercise

## Anti-Patterns
- Strategy without evidence of execution capability
- Inability to connect vision to operational reality
- Ignoring people/culture dimensions of transformation
- Over-reliance on data without strategic intuition
- Treating case as academic exercise rather than leadership demo
