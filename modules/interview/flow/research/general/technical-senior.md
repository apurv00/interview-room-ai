# General / Any Role — Technical Interview — Senior Level (7+ years)

## Topic Sequence (typical order)
1. **Technology strategy** and business alignment
2. **Architecture vision** and roadmap planning
3. **Technology evaluation** and selection methodology
4. **Balancing long-term vision** with short-term delivery
5. **Cross-team technical influence** and consensus building
6. **Security, privacy, compliance** considerations
7. **Organizational design** for engineering effectiveness
8. **Innovation and technical debt** management

## Phase Structure
- Architecture review/presentation → Strategic discussion with VP/CTO → Technical leadership scenarios → Culture fit
- Questions move beyond pure technical into leadership, negotiation, strategic communication
- May include "architecture review" presenting past system designs

## What Makes This Level Unique
- Focus shifts from "can they build" to "can they set technical direction"
- Align engineering plans with business goals
- Build coalitions through influence (not authority)
- Manage dual-track: foundational investment + short-term delivery
- Quantify trade-offs for different stakeholders
- Technology roadmaps, team structure, managing competing organizational priorities

## Anti-Patterns
- Only tech talk without business impact connection
- Inability to articulate technology vision or roadmap
- Defaulting to "it depends" without committing
- Ignoring people/org dimensions
- Over-focus on implementation vs. strategic considerations
