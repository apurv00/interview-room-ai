# General / Any Role — Behavioral Interview — Senior Level (7+ years)

## Topic Sequence (typical order)
1. **Strategic leadership** and organizational vision
2. **Culture building** and values alignment
3. **Leading through transformational change**
4. **Executive communication** and board-level influence
5. **Ethical decision-making** under business pressure
6. **Building and scaling high-performing teams**
7. **Managing resistance** to ideas/initiatives
8. **Balancing short-term delivery** with long-term strategy

## Phase Structure
- Executive panel or sequential interviews with multiple senior leaders
- Six areas: strategic alignment, team management, change management, ethical leadership, stakeholder management, cultural fit
- Heavy emphasis on "Tell me about a time you created change in a company"

## What Makes This Level Unique
- Shifts from individual/team impact to organizational impact
- Ethical leadership non-negotiable — navigating values vs. financial goals
- Must show evidence of institutional change, not just project delivery
- Demonstrate keeping teams engaged through uncertainty while maintaining strategic focus

## Anti-Patterns
- Talking about team actions rather than personal strategic decisions
- Inability to articulate a leadership philosophy
- Avoiding organizational failures or resistance discussion
- Focusing on tactical execution instead of strategic thinking
- Not connecting personal actions to business outcomes at scale
