# General / Any Role — Behavioral Interview — Entry Level (0-2 years)

## Topic Sequence (typical order)
1. **Teamwork & collaboration** in group settings
2. **Adaptability** to change and new environments
3. **Learning agility** and quick skill acquisition
4. **Conflict resolution** with peers
5. **Initiative and self-motivation**
6. **Time management** and prioritization
7. **Communication under pressure**

## Phase Structure
- Intro/rapport (3 min) → STAR-based questions (30 min) → Candidate questions (5 min)
- Accept examples from academic projects, internships, volunteer work, extracurriculars, part-time jobs

## What Makes This Level Unique
- Adaptability is #1 competency employers assess (46% of business leaders focus on it)
- No professional experience required — school/volunteer stories are valid
- Tests potential and self-awareness, not track record
- Expect 5-6 questions covering breadth of universal competencies

## Anti-Patterns
- Using "we" throughout — individual contribution undetermined
- Too vague ("I helped the team") without specifics
- 80% on Situation/Task, rushing Action/Result
- Only success stories; dodging failure looks worse than admitting mistakes
